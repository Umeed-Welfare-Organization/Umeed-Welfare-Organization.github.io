About Umeed Welfare Organization

Welcome to Umeed Welfare Organization. We are a non-profit organization dedicated to helping people in need.

The Umeed Welfare Organization, founded by Shah Amar, is a remarkable non-profit organization located in Charsadda. With a dedicated commitment to helping people, Umeed Welfare Organization focuses on providing essential assistance to those in need within the local community. Through various initiatives and programs, the organization strives to uplift the lives of individuals and families facing challenges. Led by a passionate team, Umeed Welfare Organization offers a range of services that encompass areas such as education, healthcare, and social support. Their efforts are aimed at addressing the multifaceted needs of the community and improving overall well-being. By promoting education, offering medical aid, and extending a helping hand to marginalized individuals, the organization serves as a beacon of hope for those who require assistance. The impact of Umeed Welfare Organization's work is far-reaching, creating a positive ripple effect throughout the region. Through their selfless dedication and unwavering commitment, Shah Amar and the team have proven that by coming together, people can make a significant difference in the lives of others. The organization's emphasis on social welfare serves as an inspiration for those looking to contribute to their community and make a meaningful impact.
